Help Center

Getting Started

Welcome to our Weather Application! Here's how to get started:

  • Sign up for an account or log in if you already have one.
  • Explore the "Weather Data" page to view weather conditions from various cities.
  • Use the "Add New Entry" form to contribute weather data for a city.

Viewing Weather Data

To view weather data:

  1. Go to the "Weather Data" page.
  2. You will see a table with weather information for different cities.
  3. Click the "Edit" button to modify an entry or "Delete" to remove it.

Adding Weather Data

To add weather data for a city:

  1. Go to the "Weather Data" page.
  2. Scroll down to the "Add New Entry" form.
  3. Enter the city name, temperature, and humidity.
  4. Click the "Add" button to submit the data.

Editing Weather Data

To edit weather data:

  1. Go to the "Weather Data" page.
  2. Find the entry you want to edit and click the "Edit" button.
  3. Update the information in the form and click "Update" to save your changes.
